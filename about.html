Who We Are

SED Consultants is a dynamic, client-focused consulting firm based in Vereeniging, South Africa. We specialize in helping small and medium enterprises stay compliant with CIPC, SARS, UIF, and other government regulations while offering professional accounting and tax services.

Our Mission

To empower businesses through reliable, affordable, and professional financial and statutory compliance services.

Our Vision

To be the preferred partner for South African businesses seeking seamless and trustworthy regulatory and accounting support.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We do what’s right, not what’s easy.
  • Efficiency: We meet deadlines, every time.
  • Transparency: Clear pricing, clear communication.
  • Empowerment: We educate clients so they grow stronger.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology to simplify compliance.

Meet the Team at SED Consultants

Managing Director

Seboka Dlamini

Seboka Dlamini is the founder and Managing Director of SED Consultants. With a professional background in metallurgy and engineering, and years of experience in ICT training and business development, Seboka brings a practical and people-first approach to consulting. His journey began with the launch of SED Computer Academy, where he led youth-focused initiatives that equipped hundreds of individuals with essential digital skills.

Seboka’s leadership has since expanded into the consultancy space, where he applies his technical knowledge, strategic thinking, and passion for empowerment to help businesses grow legally and sustainably.
