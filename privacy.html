Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 May 2025

Last Updated: 20 May 2025

At SED Consultants, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name, contact number, and email address
  • Company details (registration number, directors' info, etc.)
  • Tax and financial records (upon your consent)
  • Employment and payroll data for processing purposes
  • Communication preferences and queries

We collect this information directly from you when you:

  • Complete our online forms
  • Request our services via email, phone, or in-person
  • Use our website or social media pages
  • Provide supporting documents voluntarily

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Register and update business entities with CIPC
  • Perform accounting, payroll, and tax services
  • Process Workmen’s Compensation, UIF, and SARS registrations and returns
  • Contact you with updates, queries, or notices
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory authorities
  • Improve and personalise our services

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Regulatory bodies (CIPC, SARS, Department of Labour, etc.)
  • Third-party service providers (only under confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Protection and Storage

We use secure systems, password protection, and cloud storage to keep your data safe. We retain your data only as long as required by law or service purpose.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (subject to legal or contractual obligations)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

Contacting the Information Regulator

If you believe your personal information has been unlawfully accessed or processed, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.
